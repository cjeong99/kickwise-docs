Common questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Movli has no backend server, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs of any kind. Your sessions are stored on your device. If iCloud sync is on (default), they sync through your own private iCloud account — never through us, because we don't have a server to send them to. See the Privacy Policy for the full details.

Is Movli a medical device?
No. Movli is a tracking and discussion tool. It's not a substitute for professional medical advice — always follow your healthcare provider's guidance on fetal movement monitoring, and contact them directly with any concerns.

How do I turn off iCloud sync?
Settings → Data → iCloud sync.

How do I export my data?
Insights → Export PDF for doctor.
